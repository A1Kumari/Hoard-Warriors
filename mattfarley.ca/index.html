Why to use this website

The World Bank data shows that the number of persons with disabilities in India ranges between 40 and 80 million, making India a nation with the highest number of disabled people. Disability is extremely challenging and a person living with it has to fight daily battles for the most basic rights and to be included in the mainstream. Those who have a disability and come from impoverished households are even worse off and face greater difficulties in getting education, training and rehabilitation. The global COVID-19 crisis further deepened existing inequalities and exclusion, making persons with disabilities among the hardest hit.
